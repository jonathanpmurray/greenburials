Westerdale Green Burials

The cost for 2021 is £800.00.
This includes the plot in perpetuity, the digging and filling in of the grave and a stone marker tile. There is an additional cost for engraving on the stone. Biodegradable caskets are stipulated, in keeping with the environmental theme. Access is available from 9am until 7pm, or dusk (whichever is sooner). Select clothing for the weather and conditions underfoot. Car Parking space is limited. Please take this into consideration when selecting this site.
